About Us

First established in Christchurch in 1907 by the wondrous Wizen Willieotter, we are a famous New Zealand ice cream brand known for our innovative flavours and toppings, made from real ingredients fresh from their sources. We have many stores across New Zealand, and we are also renowned the world over. Many kiwis and tourists from across the globe come to our stores to try our yummy ice creams!
